Image Compressor

Welcome to the Image Compressor! This tool allows you to compress your images to reduce their file size while maintaining quality.

How to Use

  1. Click on the "Choose File" button and select an image from your device.
  2. Click on the "Compress" button to start the compression process.
  3. Once the compression is complete, you will see the original and compressed sizes along with the compression percentage.
  4. Click on the "Download Compressed Image" button to download the compressed image.

Do's and Don'ts

  • Do: Upload images in common formats like JPEG, PNG, etc.
